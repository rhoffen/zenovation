Creative Craftsmanship for Old Portland Homes

Zenovation brings creative craftsmanship to old Portland homes. We are a residential construction company specializing in the homes of inner-NE and SE Portland.

In addition to our experienced team of remodelers, we partner with reputable, licensed plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, masons, painters and sheetrock finishers.

We offer flexible payment options and tailor your project to fit your budget and your needs.
